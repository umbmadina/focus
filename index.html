about our studio

Proudly serving Setauket, Stony Brook and Port Jefferson, NY, Shine Dance Studios is a family and community oriented dance studio. Our purpose is to provide a fun and positive atmosphere to learn and express the art of dance. We offer both a recreational and a competitive class program. We offer the areas best preschool classes along with all levels and styles of dance from the beginner to the advanced. Our classes are fun yet structured and taught with proper technique. Students of all ages can gain confidence and build self esteem in our warm and inviting studio environment. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned dancer, Shine Dance Studios can help fulfill your dance education goals.
